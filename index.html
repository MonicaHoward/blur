Introducing...

postablur

The social media app designed with

social media influencers in mind first.

So·cial Me·di·a In·flu·ence·r
[soh-shuh-l mee-dee-uh in-floo-uh n-ser] - a user on social media who has established credibility in a specific industry.

A social media influencer has access to a large audience and can persuade others by virtue of their authenticity and reach.

How it works:

A postablur user uploads content to the postablur platform and adds a filter to blur out content before it is posted.

The user then sets an expiration date for the how long content will stay blurred.

Users can then specifiy the number of likes, shares, or donations needed to interurrupt the timer and reveal the content.

After the bounty for likes, shares, or donations has been met or the timer expires the content is revealed.

Once the bounty or expiration date has been reached the post will be revealed.
